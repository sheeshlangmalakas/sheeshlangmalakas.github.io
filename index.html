About Us

We are Group 6 of APEC Schools B09-10-S1-2. This group is formed under our subject, LifeLabs, that brings education and student formation to a wider scope. The aim of this subject is to offer information literacy, develop reflection, presentation, and problem solving skills, and promote 'Information and Communications Technology' competence to the students. This website will serve as a compilation and documentation of our group activities/projects throughout the whole year of LifeLabs.

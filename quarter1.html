Streaming

What is Streaming?

Streaming refers to a file such as audio and video that can be instantly played without the need to download it first. A media content, recorded or live, can be considered a streamed content as long as it can be played on any device via the internet even if you do not download it.

It works through a continuous transmission of the media content from a server to the client. That's why it is called streaming because it is delivered to viewers continuosly.

Traditional Streaming vs. Live Streaming

There are 2 types of streaming; traditional/on-demand or just simply streaming, and live streaming. On-demand streaming is a type of stream where anyone can access wether an mp3 or mp4 file anytime, anywhere. Live streaming on the other hand, is being broadcasted live and is happenning in real time(depending on both streamer and viewer's internet connection). Once the live stream is finished, the stream will be unavailable but live interaction with the streamer is lost.
